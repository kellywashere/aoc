Left column: r,c = r,1
n(r,1) = r*(r-1)/2 + 1

To find nr at (r,c):
- find number at (r + (c-1), 1) = (r+c-1)*(r+c-2)/2 + 1
- add (c-1) to it

n(r,c) = (r+c-1)*(r+c-2)/2 + c
